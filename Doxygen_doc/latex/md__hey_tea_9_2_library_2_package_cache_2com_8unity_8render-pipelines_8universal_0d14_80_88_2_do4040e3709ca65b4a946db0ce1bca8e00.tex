The {\bfseries{Lens Distortion}} effect distorts the final rendered picture to simulate the shape of a real-\/world camera lens.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_do4040e3709ca65b4a946db0ce1bca8e00_autotoc_md2299}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Using Lens Distortion}{Using Lens Distortion}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_do4040e3709ca65b4a946db0ce1bca8e00_autotoc_md2299}
{\bfseries{Lens Distortion}} uses the \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_documentation_0i_2_volumes}{Volume}{0} framework, so to enable and modify {\bfseries{Lens Distortion}} properties, you must add a {\bfseries{Lens Distortion}} override to a \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_documentation_0i_2_volumes}{Volume}{0} in your Scene. To add {\bfseries{Lens Distortion}} to a Volume\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item In the Scene or Hierarchy view, select a Game\+Object that contains a Volume component to view it in the Inspector.
\item In the Inspector, navigate to {\bfseries{Add Override \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} Post-\/processing}} and click on {\bfseries{Lens Distortion}}. URP now applies {\bfseries{Lens Distortion}} to any Camera this Volume affects.
\end{DoxyEnumerate}\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_do4040e3709ca65b4a946db0ce1bca8e00_autotoc_md2300}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Properties}{Properties}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_do4040e3709ca65b4a946db0ce1bca8e00_autotoc_md2300}
