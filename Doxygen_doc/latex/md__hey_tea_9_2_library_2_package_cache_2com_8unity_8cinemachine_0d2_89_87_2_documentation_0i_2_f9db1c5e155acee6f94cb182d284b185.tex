 This Virtual Camera {\bfseries{Body}} algorithm moves the camera in a fixed screen-\/space relationship to the {\bfseries{Follow}} target. You can also specify offsets, damping, and composition rules. {\bfseries{Framing Transposer}} only changes the camera’s position in space. It does not re-\/orient or otherwise aim the camera.

{\bfseries{Framing Transposer}} is designed for 2D and orthographic cameras. But it works also with perspective cameras and 3D environments.

This algorithm first moves the camera along the camera Z axis until the {\bfseries{Follow}} target is at the desired distance from the camera’s X-\/Y plane. It then moves the camera in its X-\/Y plane until the {\bfseries{Follow}} target is at the desired point on the camera’s screen.

{\bfseries{Note}}\+: {\bfseries{Framing Transposer}} ignores the Look\+At target -\/ only the Follow target is used.
