\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A component for making a child Rect\+Transform scroll. 

\doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_scroll_rect}{Scroll\+Rect} will not do any clipping on its own. Combined with a \doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_mask}{Mask} component, it can be turned into a scroll view.
