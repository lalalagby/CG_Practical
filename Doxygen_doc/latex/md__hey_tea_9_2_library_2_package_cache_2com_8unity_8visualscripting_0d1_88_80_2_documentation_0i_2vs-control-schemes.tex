Pan to move the viewable area in the Graph Editor to any part of your graph.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5197}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Pan Vertically}{Pan Vertically}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5197}
With the {\bfseries{Default}} control scheme, pan the view in the Graph Editor vertically with the scroll wheel on your mouse.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5198}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Zoom In/\+Zoom Out}{Zoom In/\+Zoom Out}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5198}
Change the zoom level in the Graph window to control how much of your graph is visible in the Graph Editor.

You can also set your zoom level with the toolbar in the Graph window. For more information, see \href{vs-interface-overview.md\#the-graph-toolbar}{\texttt{ The interface}}.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5199}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Frame Selected}{Frame Selected}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5199}
After you select a node or another item in your graph, press Home to center your selected item in the Graph Editor.

\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5200}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Frame All}{Frame All}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5200}
With no nodes or items selected, press Home to center your entire graph in the Graph Editor. Your zoom level automatically adjusts to accommodate the size of your graph.

\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5201}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Create Selection}{Create Selection}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5201}
Click and drag to create a selection box around any nodes or items in your graph that you want to select.



When you have multiple items selected, click and drag a single item to move the entire selection.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5202}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Select All}{Select All}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5202}
Press Ctrl+A (mac\+OS\+: Cmd+A) to select all items in your current graph.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5203}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Open Context Menu}{Open Context Menu}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5203}
You can open the context menu to perform certain actions on State Graphs or manipulate a selection in a Script Graph. You can create new states and add transitions.

Paste the contents of a copied or cut selection into your graph. \hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5207}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Cut Selected}{Cut Selected}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5207}
Cut your current selection to move it to another graph, or another location on your current graph.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5208}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Duplicate Selected}{Duplicate Selected}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8visualscripting_0d1_88_80_2_documentation_0i_2vs-control-schemes_autotoc_md5208}
Duplicate a selection to instantly create a copy of your current selection to use elsewhere in your current graph.

You can move a group in your graph without any of the nodes contained inside that group.
