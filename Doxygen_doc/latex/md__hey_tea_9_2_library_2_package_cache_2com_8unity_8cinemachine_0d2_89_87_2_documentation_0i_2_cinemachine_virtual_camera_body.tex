\item \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8cinemachine_0d2_89_87_2_documentation_0i_2_cinemachine3rd_person_follow}{{\bfseries{3rd Person follow}}}{0}\+: Pivots the camera horizontally and vertically around the player, to the {\bfseries{Follow}} target.
