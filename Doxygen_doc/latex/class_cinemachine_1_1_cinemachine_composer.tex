\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This is a Cinemachine\+Component in the Aim section of the component pipeline. Its job is to aim the camera at the vcam\textquotesingle{}s Look\+At target object, with configurable offsets, damping, and composition rules. 

The composer does not change the camera\textquotesingle{}s position. It will only pan and tilt the camera where it is, in order to get the desired framing. To move the camera, you have to use the virtual camera\textquotesingle{}s Body section.
