\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This is a Cinemachine\+Component in the Body section of the component pipeline. Its job is to position the camera in a fixed relationship to the vcam\textquotesingle{}s Follow target object, with offsets and damping. 

The Tansposer will only change the camera\textquotesingle{}s position in space. It will not re-\/orient or otherwise aim the camera. To to that, you need to instruct the vcam in the Aim section of its pipeline.
