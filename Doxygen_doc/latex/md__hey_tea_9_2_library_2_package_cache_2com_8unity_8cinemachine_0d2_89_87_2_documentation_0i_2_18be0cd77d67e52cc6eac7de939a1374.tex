\doxylink{namespace_cinemachine}{Cinemachine} Collision Impulse Source

For collision-\/based impulses, attach a {\bfseries{Cinemachine\+Collision\+Impulse\+Source}} component to a Game\+Object that has a Collider or Collider2D component. {\bfseries{Cinemachine\+Collision\+Impulse\+Source}} generates an impulse when something collides with the Game\+Object or enters a trigger zone.

To add a Collision Impulse Source component\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Select the Game\+Object that you want to trigger impulses, navigate to its Inspector, and click the {\bfseries{Add Component}} button.
\item Go to {\bfseries{Scripts \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} \doxylink{namespace_cinemachine}{Cinemachine}}} and select {\bfseries{\doxylink{namespace_cinemachine}{Cinemachine} Collision Impulse Source}}.
\end{DoxyEnumerate}

By default, an Impulse Source affects every \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8cinemachine_0d2_89_87_2_documentation_0i_2_cinemachine_impulse_listener}{Impulse Listener}{0} in range, but you can apply \href{CinemachineImpulseFiltering.md\#ChannelFiltering}{\texttt{ channel filtering}} to make Impulse Sources affect some Impulse Listeners and not others.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8cinemachine_0d2_89_87_2_documentation_0i_2_18be0cd77d67e52cc6eac7de939a1374_autotoc_md667}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Properties\+:}{Properties\+:}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8cinemachine_0d2_89_87_2_documentation_0i_2_18be0cd77d67e52cc6eac7de939a1374_autotoc_md667}

An impulse is triggered at a location by an impact mass moving at an impact velocity. \doxylink{namespace_unity}{Unity} dynamically calculates the mass and velocity of the Rigidbody or Rigidbody 2D component that triggers the impulse. The {\bfseries{How To Generate The Impulse}} properties control how the mass and velocity of the Rigidbody affect the strength and direction of the signal.
