The Bloom effect creates fringes of light extending from the borders of bright areas in an image. This creates the illusion of extremely bright light overwhelming the Camera.

The Bloom effect also has a {\bfseries{Lens Dirt}} feature, which you can use to apply a full-\/screen layer of smudges or dust to diffract the Bloom effect.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_do6c59edf4ad2c229be0b36e477eb69ba3_autotoc_md2272}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Using Bloom}{Using Bloom}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_do6c59edf4ad2c229be0b36e477eb69ba3_autotoc_md2272}
{\bfseries{Bloom}} uses the \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_documentation_0i_2_volumes}{Volume}{0} system, so to enable and modify {\bfseries{Bloom}} properties, you must add a {\bfseries{Bloom}} override to a \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_do5d8e84fb42b2bb0720761c155081d9b4}{Volume}{0} in your Scene.

To add {\bfseries{Bloom}} to a Volume\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item In the Scene or Hierarchy view, select a Game\+Object that contains a Volume component to view it in the Inspector.
\item In the Inspector, navigate to {\bfseries{Add Override}} \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} {\bfseries{Post-\/processing}}, and click on {\bfseries{Bloom}}. Universal Render Pipeline applies {\bfseries{Bloom}} to any Camera this Volume affects.

There are multiple ways to improve the performance impact of Bloom. Listed in order of effectiveness, you can\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Disable {\bfseries{High Quality Filtering}}. Bloom then uses bilinear filtering instead of bicubic. This reduces the overall smoothness of the Bloom effect, but greatly improves performance, especially on lower-\/end hardware and platforms. In some extreme cases, you might see blocky graphical artifacts in your Scene.
\item Set {\bfseries{Downscale}} to {\bfseries{Quarter}} starting resolution to make the initial cost of Bloom much lower.
\item Use a lower resolution {\bfseries{Lens Dirt}} Texture to reduce memory pressure and speed up blending across volumes. 
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
