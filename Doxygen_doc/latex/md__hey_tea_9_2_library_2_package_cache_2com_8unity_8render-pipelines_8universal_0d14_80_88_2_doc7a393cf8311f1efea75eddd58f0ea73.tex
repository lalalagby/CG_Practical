{\bfseries{Color Adjustments}} uses the \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_documentation_0i_2_volumes}{Volume}{0} framework, so to enable and modify {\bfseries{Color Adjustments}} properties, you must add a {\bfseries{Color Adjustments}} override to a \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8render-pipelines_8universal_0d14_80_88_2_documentation_0i_2_volumes}{Volume}{0} in your Scene. To add {\bfseries{Color Adjustments}} to a Volume\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item In the Scene or Hierarchy view, select a Game\+Object that contains a Volume component to view it in the Inspector.
\item In the Inspector, navigate to {\bfseries{Add Override \texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} Post-\/processing}} and click on {\bfseries{Color Adjustments}}. URP now applies {\bfseries{Color Adjustments}} to any Camera this Volume affects.
