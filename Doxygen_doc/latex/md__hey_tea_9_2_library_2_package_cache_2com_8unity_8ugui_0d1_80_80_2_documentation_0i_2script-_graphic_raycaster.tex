 The Graphic Raycaster is used to raycast against a Canvas. The Raycaster looks at all \doxylink{namespace_graphics}{Graphics} on the canvas and determines if any of them have been hit.

The Graphic Raycaster can be configured to ignore backfacing \doxylink{namespace_graphics}{Graphics} as well as be blocked by 2D or 3D objects that exist in front of it. A manual priority can also be applied if you want processing of this element to be forced to the front or back of the Raycasting.\hypertarget{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8ugui_0d1_80_80_2_documentation_0i_2script-_graphic_raycaster_autotoc_md4871}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Properties}{Properties}}\label{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8ugui_0d1_80_80_2_documentation_0i_2script-_graphic_raycaster_autotoc_md4871}
