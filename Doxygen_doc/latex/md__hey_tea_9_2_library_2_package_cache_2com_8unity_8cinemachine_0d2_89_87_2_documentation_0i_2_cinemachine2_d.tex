 \doxylink{namespace_cinemachine}{Cinemachine} supports orthographic cameras. When you set the \doxylink{namespace_unity}{Unity} camera\textquotesingle{}s projection to Orthographic, \doxylink{namespace_cinemachine}{Cinemachine} adjusts to accommodate it. In Virtual Camera properties for {\bfseries{Lens}}, {\bfseries{FOV}} is replaced by {\bfseries{Orthographic Size}}. Note that settings related to FOV and certain FOV-\/oriented behaviors such as \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8cinemachine_0d2_89_87_2_documentation_0i_2_cinemachine_follow_zoom}{Follow Zoom}{0} have no effect if the camera is orthographic.
