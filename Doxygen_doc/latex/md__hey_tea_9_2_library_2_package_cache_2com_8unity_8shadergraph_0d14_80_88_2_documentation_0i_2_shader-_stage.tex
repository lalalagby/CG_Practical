{\bfseries{Shader Stage}} refers to the part of the shader pipeline a \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8shadergraph_0d14_80_88_2_documentation_0i_2_node}{Node}{0} or \doxysectlink{md__hey_tea_9_2_library_2_package_cache_2com_8unity_8shadergraph_0d14_80_88_2_documentation_0i_2_port}{Port}{0} is part of. For example, {\bfseries{Vertex}} or {\bfseries{Fragment}}.

In Shader Graph, {\bfseries{Shader Stage}} is defined per port but often all ports on a node are locked to the same {\bfseries{Shader Stage}}. Ports on some nodes are unavailable in certain {\bfseries{Shader Stages}} due to limitations in the underlying shader language.
