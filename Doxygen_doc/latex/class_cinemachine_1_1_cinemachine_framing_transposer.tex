\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This is a \doxylink{namespace_cinemachine}{Cinemachine} Component in the Body section of the component pipeline. Its job is to position the camera in a fixed screen-\/space relationship to the vcam\textquotesingle{}s Follow target object, with offsets and damping. 

The camera will be first moved along the camera Z axis until the Follow target is at the desired distance from the camera\textquotesingle{}s X-\/Y plane. The camera will then be moved in its XY plane until the Follow target is at the desired point on the camera\textquotesingle{}s screen.

The Framing\+Tansposer will only change the camera\textquotesingle{}s position in space. It will not re-\/orient or otherwise aim the camera.

For this component to work properly, the vcam\textquotesingle{}s Look\+At target must be null. The Follow target will define what the camera is looking at.

If the Follow target is a \doxylink{interface_cinemachine_1_1_i_cinemachine_target_group}{ICinemachine\+Target\+Group}, then additional controls will be available to dynamically adjust the camera\textquotesingle{}s view in order to frame the entire group.

Although this component was designed for orthographic cameras, it works equally well with persective cameras and can be used in 3D environments.
