\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This is a Cinemachine\+Component in the Aim section of the component pipeline. Its job is to aim the camera at a target object, with configurable offsets, damping, and composition rules. 

In addition, if the target is a \doxylink{interface_cinemachine_1_1_i_cinemachine_target_group}{ICinemachine\+Target\+Group}, the behaviour will adjust the FOV and the camera distance to ensure that the entire group of targets is framed properly.
