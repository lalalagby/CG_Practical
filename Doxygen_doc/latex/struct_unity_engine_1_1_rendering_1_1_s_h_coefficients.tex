\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Contains spherical harmonic coefficients used for lighting representation in the format expected by {\ttfamily DOTS\+\_\+\+INSTANCING\+\_\+\+ON} shaders. 

The size of the struct is padded to a power of two so arrays of such structs can be efficiently indexed in shaders.
