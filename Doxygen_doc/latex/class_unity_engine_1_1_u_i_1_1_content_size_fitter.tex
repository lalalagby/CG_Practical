\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Resizes a Rect\+Transform to fit the size of its content. 

The \doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_content_size_fitter}{Content\+Size\+Fitter} can be used on Game\+Objects that have one or more \doxylink{interface_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_i_layout_element}{ILayout\+Element} components, such as \doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_text}{Text}, \doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_image}{Image}, \doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_horizontal_layout_group}{Horizontal\+Layout\+Group}, \doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_vertical_layout_group}{Vertical\+Layout\+Group}, and \doxylink{class_unity_engine_1_1_u_i_1_1_grid_layout_group}{Grid\+Layout\+Group}.
